1. Privacy Policy & Data Security

Your privacy is our highest priority. NoteYou operates on a strict Zero-Knowledge Architecture.

1.1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register for cloud sync, we collect your email address to create and manage your account via Google Firebase.
  • Device & Diagnostics Data: To improve app stability, we may automatically collect anonymous diagnostic data (e.g., app crash logs, device model, operating system version). This data contains no personally identifiable information and no note content.
  • Content Data (Encrypted): If you use the sync feature, the encrypted ciphertext of your notes is stored on our servers. We do not collect or store unencrypted note data.

1.2. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) Mechanism

  • How It Works: When you secure your account, you create a personal Encryption PIN. Our app generates a cryptographic 'Salt' stored on our servers. Your local Encryption PIN combined with this Server Salt generates a secure Master Key directly on your device.
  • Zero-Knowledge Proof: Your Encryption PIN is NEVER transmitted to, nor stored on, our servers. When you log out, the PIN and the Master Key are permanently wiped from your device's local memory.
  • Absolute Privacy: Because we do not have your PIN, we cannot generate your Master Key. It is mathematically and technically impossible for us, our developers, or any third party to access, read, decrypt, or share your notes.

1.3. Incorrect PIN & Decryption Failures

  • If an incorrect Encryption PIN is entered, the system will mathematically generate an invalid Master Key. Consequently, your notes will fail to decrypt.
  • Because we do not store your PIN, the app cannot verify or notify you if the PIN is "wrong" prior to decryption. If an incorrect PIN is provided, your notes will simply not appear, or will remain as unreadable encrypted text. This is an intentional security design, not a software bug.

1.4. Biometrics & Local Storage

  • App Lock: If enabled, fingerprint or facial recognition data is processed entirely offline by your device's native hardware OS (Android). NoteYou does not collect, transmit, or store biometric data.

1.5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase for secure authentication and to host the encrypted ciphertext. Firebase infrastructure complies with strict industry security standards.
  • Razorpay: Premium payments are processed securely by Razorpay. We do not collect, process, or store your credit/debit card numbers, UPI IDs, or sensitive banking details.
